WE NEED TO SAFEGUARD OUR FORESTS

The importance of forests cannot be underestimated.We depend on forests for
our survival, from the air we breathe to the wood we use. Besides providing
habitats for animals and livelihoods for humans, forests also offer watershed
protection,prevent soil erosion and mitigate climate change. Yet, despite our
dependence on forests, we are still allowing them to disappear.
